Welcome to Villa Designs

Who are Villa Designs?

We are an independent business born out of our love for crafting beautiful and distinctive handcrafted wooden furniture and gifts.

Our Story:

We met in 2019 and quickly realised that we had a lot in common. Both our relationship and the business flourished through our shared passion for working with wood. We spend all our free time in the workshop, with our trusted companion Rocket, the black lab, who was, of course, our inspiration for our logo!

We are absolute enthusiasts when it comes to creating quality items with our hands and are both trained carpenters.

The sustainable use of timber is incredibly important to us. Wherever possible, we use English hardwoods that can be traced back to a manageable source.
